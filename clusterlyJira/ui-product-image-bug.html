UI showing same image for all recommended products

Medium Priority
Description

Bug Summary: In the "Recommended for You" section (Cart and Product pages), all product items are displaying default placeholder images instead of their actual product images. The system is showing generic camera/placeholder icons rather than the intended product thumbnails.

Expected Behavior: Each recommended product should display its actual product image corresponding to the specific item (kitchen oven, kurtha collection, running shoes, smartphone).

Actual Behavior: All 4 recommended products show default placeholder/camera icons instead of their respective product images.
